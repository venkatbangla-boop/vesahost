What does VESA Enterprise Inc do?

VESA Enterprise Inc supports apparel sourcing, product development, factory matching, production management, quality assurance and supply chain execution across Bangladesh and India.

Where does VESA source garments?

VESA focuses on Bangladesh and India apparel production platforms for global retailers, importers and brands.

What garment categories does VESA support?

VESA supports men's, ladies' and kids' apparel categories including knits, wovens, polos, tees, casualwear, activewear, dresses, bottoms and kidswear.

What is EGENVA?

EGENVA is the intelligence platform used to support sourcing workflows, supplier intelligence, market intelligence, knowledge management and decision support.
